The World of Pharloom

Pharloom is not just a new kingdom‚Äîit's a place of pilgrimage, filled with religious undertones and mysterious rulers. Unlike Hallownest, which was crumbling into decay, Pharloom is vibrant, radiant, and alive, but its beauty hides deadly trials. Its shining citadel sits high above the land, symbolizing both hope and danger for Hornet's journey.

Pharloom is divided into many unique regions, each with its own enemies, music, and atmosphere: Coral Forests glowing with strange lights, Industrial towns powered by clockwork machinery, Silken caves filled with traps and enemies wrapped in threads, and Sacred temples that hint at Pharloom's divine mystery.

Armed with her needle and thread, Hornet discovers that Pharloom holds secrets that could reshape the very fabric of reality. The kingdom pulses with a mysterious energy, and its inhabitants speak of a great ritual that could either save or doom all who dwell within its silken walls.

Character Overview

Hornet, known in Hollow Knight as a fierce warrior and protector of Hallownest, becomes the heroine of this story. Unlike the Knight, she speaks, giving the sequel a more narrative-driven feel.

Needle & Thread

Her signature weapon is both a blade and a tool. She can stitch enemies, swing across gaps, and pull herself into battle with incredible precision and grace.

Agility & Acrobatics

Hornet is faster and more graceful, reflecting her royal upbringing and combat training. Her movements are fluid and deadly.

Personality

Bold, determined, and sharp-tongued, Hornet adds more character depth than the silent Knight ever did, bringing personality to every encounter.
